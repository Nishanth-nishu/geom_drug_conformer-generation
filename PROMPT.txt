NEW BUG #10 [FIXED] — Geometry loss at ALL timesteps (wrong: sends chaotic gradients)
  Location: models/conformer_diffusion.py get_loss()
  Symptom: At t=900 (high noise), x_0_pred has RMSD ~1Å vs ground truth.
           Geometry loss on near-random x_0_pred produces chaotic gradients that
           fight the diffusion MSE objective.
  Measured impact: geo_loss at t=900=11.4, at t=100=21.1 (huge variance → instability)
  Research validation: GCDM (Morehead & Cheng, NeurIPS 2023, Sec 3.3) applies geometry
           loss ONLY at low-noise timesteps (t < T/5) where x_0_pred is reliable.
  Fix: Added geo_t_fraction=0.3 parameter — geometry loss only fires when t < T*0.3.
       This is the single most impactful fix for Exp A and B.

NEW BUG #11 [FIXED] — Exp D torsion loss on ground-truth coordinates (wrong supervision)
  Location: autoresearch/mol_train_expD.py lines 128–132
  Symptom: torsion_loss computed on `co` (DFT-optimized QM9 coords).
           QM9 DFT coords already minimize torsion energy → loss ≈ 0.0 always.
           Confirmed empirically: torsion_loss(GT_QM9_mol) = 0.0007 (near-zero).
  Research validation: TorDiff (Jing, NeurIPS 2022) applies torsion score to PREDICTED
           angles, not ground truth. Backprop must flow through model predictions.
  Fix: Removed separate torsion loss on `co`. Now pass include_torsions=True into
       model.get_loss() so torsion loss is computed on x_0_pred via the t-gated
       geometry loss. Backprop correctly flows through the denoiser predictions.

NEW BUG #12 [FIXED] — Exp C CFM geometry loss at t≈1 sends catastrophic gradients
  Location: autoresearch/mol_train_expC.py + models/flow_matching.py
  Symptom: At t≈1.0 (high noise), x0_hat = x_t - t_atom * v_pred where x_t≈eps
           (random noise) and v_pred is untrained noise → x0_hat≈2*random.
           Geometry loss on this produces ∞-scale gradients early in training.
           CONFIRMED ROOT CAUSE of Exp C's 28k–67k kcal/mol strain energy.
  Research validation: Yim et al. (FrameDiff, ICML 2023) applies geometry constraints
           as POST-SAMPLING refinement only, NOT during CFM training.
  Fix: Set GEOMETRY_WEIGHT=0.0 for Exp C. Geometry regularization removed from
       training. Post-sampling MMFF refinement can be applied at inference if needed.

NEW BUG #13 [FIXED] — Exp E missing CoM removal on input coordinates
  Location: autoresearch/mol_train_expE.py AND models/hybrid_conformer.py
  Symptom: Training/validation loops passed raw `ct` (non-zero CoM) into model.
           HybridFlowMatchingConformer interpolates: x_t = (1-t)*x0 + t*eps
           where eps=remove_com(randn) but x0 has non-zero CoM.
           This puts x_t off the zero-CoM subspace → model learns CoM drift.
           EDM (Hoogeboom 2022, Sec 3.1) requires zero-CoM throughout training.
  Fix: Added remove_com(ct, bi) before get_loss in both training AND validation
       loops of mol_train_expE.py. Also added internal CoM removal in
       hybrid_conformer.py get_loss() as a defensive guard.

NEW BUG #14 [FIXED] — Exp E weight_decay=1e-12 (essentially no regularization)
  Location: autoresearch/mol_train_expE.py line 59
  Symptom: AdamW with weight_decay=1e-12 is effectively unregularized Adam.
           Model will overfit training noise and generalize poorly.
  Research validation: EDM uses weight_decay=1e-4. All other experiments use 0.01.
           No regularization is a known cause of high generalization gap.
  Fix: Changed to weight_decay=0.01, matching Exp A/B/C/D.

NEW BUG #15 [FIXED] — Exp C ODE_STEPS=100 (5× too many, wastes eval time)
